Trees

A tree is a plant that can grow in height and has branches and leaves.

Below are 5 facts about trees:

  1. Trees have been on Earth for 370 million years.
  2. The California Redwood is the tallest tree on Earth.
  3. There are over 60,000 species of trees.
  4. You can count the tree rings to find out how old it is!
  5. Branches are there to spread the leaves out.
